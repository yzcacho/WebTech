Culture Appreciation

This advocacy aims to teach the youth to know more about the diversty of cultures in Bakakeng Central. Bakakeng Central is known to be having more than one culture living in the premises. So, residents, especially children, have a broader perspective in knowing about each culture. Also, this advocacy teaches the next generation on how to play various local instruments like the gongs.

Heritage Preservation

This advocacy intends to help residents know more about on how to preserve their own culture and on how to help others preserve theirs.

'Ipakita ang tunay na IKAW!'

This is an activity wherein residents of different cultures showcase their respective cultural dances.
