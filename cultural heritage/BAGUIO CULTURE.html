Baguio has been known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, due to its fine weather making it a perfect place to relax and enjoy the activities held there and visit some of its famous landmarks such as the Botanical Garden and one of the biggest events here in Baguio is the Panagbenga
Baguio has been known for its rich culture. It has provided one of the finest products such as Strawberries and Jams, Handweavings, Furniture and Wood Carvings. Baguio known as the "Cordillera Gateway" the melting pot of the Cordillera tribe also known as the "Igorots" have different cultural dances and traditions that preserves their culture such as canao which is being practiced during weddings, feasts, and death of a member of the tribe
