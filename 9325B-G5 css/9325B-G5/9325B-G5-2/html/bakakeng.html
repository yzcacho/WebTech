Bakakeng

Barangay Bakakeng is one of the best barangay's here in Baguio, known for it's restaurants and its kind residents. It is also one of the most widest and populated areas here in Baguio.

Bakakeng has a variety of traditions and cultures. One tradition in Bakakeng is teaching the children how to use different instruments such as gongs and the sulibao, in preparation to their general assembly which is held at the barangay hall. They also conduct cultural dances during their general assembly which is performed by mostly children and adults. These are usually held twice a year during the first week of March and October.
